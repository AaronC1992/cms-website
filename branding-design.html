Our Branding Process

Strategic, collaborative, and built to deliver a brand you are proud to put your name on.

1

Brand Discovery

We start with a deep-dive session covering your vision, values, target audience, competitors, and the feeling you want your brand to evoke.

2

Concept Development

Our designers develop three distinct creative directions, each grounded in strategy and your brand brief, presented with rationale.

3

Refinement & Presentation

We refine your chosen direction through collaborative feedback rounds until every detail is exactly right.

4

Finalize & Package

All final assets are packaged and delivered in every format you need, including print-ready, web-ready, and source files.

5

Brand Rollout Support

We help you apply the new brand across your key touchpoints, from your website and social profiles to signage and packaging.
